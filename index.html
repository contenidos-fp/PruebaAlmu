1) Upper maxilla:

There are two bones, connected by the median palatine raphe, which comprised the alveolar processes and the upper arch teeth. They are a fixed bone component of the stomatognathic system and the most part of the upper facial skeleton.

  • Regular bone.
  • The regular bone articulates with the frontal, ethmoid, palatine, malar, unguis, nasal, vomer, and inferior nasal concha.
  • It is a part of the palatine dome, the nasal fossae, and the orbit.
  • It contains the maxillary sinus.

  • Canine eminence
  • Anterior nasal spine
